# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: 12-05-2026**

Welcome to the Swastika Jan Kalyan Foundation website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

## 1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. We only collect personal information when you explicitly provide it to us to join as a volunteer or make a donation. The information collected may include:

- Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
- Postal Address and Permanent Account Number (PAN).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly used for the following purposes:

- **Donations & Compliance:** We collect your Address and PAN exclusively because it is a mandatory legal requirement under the Income Tax Act for us to issue your Form 10BE (80G) tax exemption certificate.
- **Volunteering:** To process your application and communicate with you regarding our environment, education, and sanitation initiatives.
- **Communication:** To send you updates regarding your donation receipts and certificates.

## 3. Cookies

We respect your digital privacy. Our website does not use, collect, or store any cookies or tracking pixels.

## 4. Third-Party Payment Processing

For seamless and secure donations, we have integrated Razorpay as our payment gateway. When you make a donation, your payment details (such as credit/debit card numbers or UPI IDs) are processed directly by Razorpay through their secure servers. We do not store or have access to your payment credentials. We recommend reviewing Razorpay's Privacy Policy to understand their data handling practices.

## 5. Data Sharing and Security

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data (like PAN and Address) is only shared with the Income Tax Department of India for the sole purpose of statutory compliance and issuing your 80G certificates.
